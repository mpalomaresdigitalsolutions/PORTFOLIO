Google Ads Campaign Optimization: A Case Study

Transforming Challenges into Quantifiable Success for Humanity Impact Institute Org

The Challenge: Strategic Campaign Restructuring

Humanity Impact Institute Org faced critical inefficiencies in their Google Ads campaign architecture. The account exhibited fundamental targeting misalignment, with ads serving to geographically irrelevant audiences and keyword strategies attracting non-qualified traffic. Most critically, their conversion tracking methodology was fundamentally flawed - measuring donation volume rather than internship application submissions, creating a complete disconnect between campaign performance and actual business objectives. This misalignment resulted in substantial budget waste and prevented accurate performance assessment, necessitating a comprehensive strategic overhaul to align digital spend with organizational mission outcomes.

Strategic Campaign Optimization

Conducted comprehensive account audit revealing systemic inefficiencies across targeting, keyword strategy, and conversion attribution. Implemented data-driven restructuring including refined audience segmentation, precision keyword optimization with negative keyword expansion, and device-level bid adjustments. Established proper conversion tracking architecture aligned to internship application submissions, enabling accurate ROI measurement and strategic decision-making.
