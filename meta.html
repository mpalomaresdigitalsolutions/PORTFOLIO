Meta Ads Strategy

Hook: Scale profitable Meta Ads with data-driven creative testing and precision audience targeting. Turn scrolls into conversions.

This showcase demonstrates a performance-focused Meta (Facebook) Ads approach. We combine structured creative testing, optimized delivery, and audience refinement to consistently improve CPA and conversion quality. Below are snapshots of campaign results and creative iterations used to achieve scalable performance.

Results snapshot: improved ROAS and reduced CPA through audience refinement and creative testing.
Creative iteration: high-performing hooks and value-driven angles tested across lookalike and interest audiences.
Mirror Local Business: hyperlocal targeting + creative that feels native to the feed, tuned for efficient acquisition. Results: 47% lower CPA and 3.2x ROAS improvement.

Here’s exactly how I got these results, smart and simple. I start with a fast audit to align offer, creative and tracking. Pixel + Conversion API are verified (events deduped), then I set up structured tests: 3–5 hooks × 2 visuals × 2 CTAs. The goal is to isolate what moves CPA while keeping delivery stable.

Audiences are layered for signal strength: stacked interests to find broad patterns, then Lookalikes from high-quality events (purchases, add‑to‑cart, engaged video viewers). I use ABO when I need control and CBO once winners emerge, with budgets scaled smoothly so the algorithm doesn’t reset learning.

Creatives stay native to the feed—UGC style, clear value, and quick proof. I rotate angles (problem → value → proof → CTA), keep first 2 seconds thumb‑stopping, and make the CTA feel low‑friction. On the landing side, I tighten the path: relevant headline, social proof near the fold, and a single conversion action.

Optimization cadence is weekly: trim high‑CPA ad sets, duplicate winners to new budgets, refresh creatives before fatigue, and update hooks from comments and search terms. Retargeting is simple and effective: engaged visitors, video viewers, and cart abandoners get tailored follow‑ups with short deadlines and proof.

Net effect: more qualified impressions, cleaner delivery, and stable scaling. It feels casual on the surface, but behind the scenes everything is measured and iterated so results compound week over week.
